Data Collection

we specialize in gathering, analyzing, and delivering extensive datasets to help you make informed decisions. We meticulously collect data from a variety of authoritative sources, including:

MCA

MCA (Ministry of Corporate Affairs) oversees corporate governance, company laws, and regulations in India.

GST

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a comprehensive indirect tax on the supply of goods and services in India.

MSME

MSME (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises) are businesses in India that contribute significantly to economic growth and employment.

IEC

IEC (Import Export Code) is a unique identification number required for businesses engaged in import and export in India.

STARTUP

A startup is a young company founded to develop a unique product or service and bring it to market, often characterized by innovation and scalability.

COURTS

Courts are judicial institutions that interpret and apply the law to resolve disputes and administer justice.

BUREAU

A bureau is a government department or agency responsible for specific administrative functions or services.

MARKET & NEWS

Market & News refers to information and analysis related to economic trends, financial activities, and current events impacting various industries and sectors

CREDIT RATING

Credit rating is an assessment of an individual's or entity's creditworthiness based on their financial history and ability to repay debt obligations.

EPFO

EPFO (Employees' Provident Fund Organisation) manages social security funds and benefits like provident fund, pension, and insurance for employees in India

PAN

PAN (Permanent Account Number) is a unique 10-character alphanumeric identifier issued by the Income Tax Department in India for taxpayers.

REGULATORS

Regulators are governmental or non-governmental bodies responsible for overseeing and enforcing rules, standards, and practices within specific industries or sectors.
